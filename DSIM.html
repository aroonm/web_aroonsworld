Draw Something for iMessage

The problem with mobile games these days is that they aren't social anymore. You get to play with people on your Facebook friend list, but most people on this list who happen to play the same game are rarely people you even know. In contrast, iMessage being a Messaging platform, encourages players to start 'meaningful' game sessions with people they interact with the most. I find this exciting because it indirectly creates game sessions that do not necessarily have to end, games with 100% retention.
